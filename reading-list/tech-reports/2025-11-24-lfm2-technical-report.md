Second generation of Liquid Foundation Models (LFMs), optimized explicitly for on-device deployment.

Contains:
- Hardware-in-the-loop architecture search for on-device efficiency
- A novel knowledge distillation objective for small model training
- Three-stage post-training recipes that work at small scales
- `LFM2-VL` for vision-language understanding, `LFM2-Audio` for real-time speech interaction, `LFM2-ColBERT` for multilingual retrieval
